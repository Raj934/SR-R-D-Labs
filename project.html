SKS (Synchronous Key System)

Overview: Say Goodbye to Lost Keys and Security Worries! Introducing the Synchronous Key System. This innovative system combines the familiarity of a traditional key with the power of advanced technology. It offers enhanced security through multi-factor authentication and a convenient digital key backup on your phone, all while maintaining user-friendliness.

Features and Benefits:

  • Enhanced Security: Multi-factor authentication through a physical key with RFID verification, optional digital key with user authentication, and a blocked keyhole significantly improves security compared to traditional methods.
  • Convenience: Combines the familiarity of a physical key with the ease of a digital backup on your phone, eliminating the risk of lockouts due to lost keys or dead batteries.
  • Offline Functionality (Optional): The digital key can work even without an internet connection, ensuring access even in situations with limited mobile signal.
  • Potential Applications: Suitable for homes, apartments, offices, short-term rentals, and high-security facilities.
  • Future-Proof Design: Can integrate with smart home platforms and explore advanced features like biometric authentication for even stronger security.

Market Potential:

  • Residential Security Solutions
  • Commercial and Office Buildings
  • Short-term Rentals and Hospitality

Status:

Currently in development phase, targeting release in Q4 2024.
